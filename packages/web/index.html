Cesare Emiliani proposed in 1993 to add 10,000 more years to the current date for a better grasping of our human civilization, to have a Holocene Epoch.

The Holocene Epoch Timestamp grants the user with the possibility of making the conversion easy between the two types of date. The browser extension can be used to automatically convert the timestamps from the web page.
